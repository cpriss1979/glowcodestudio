Fit to Fab

A lightweight, mobile-first wellness mini-app to explore smart food choices, track simple workouts, and log your progress — fast and private.

  • Sections: Low-Calorie Foods, so-called "Negative-Calorie" Foods*, Exercises, Calorie Calculator, Personal Progress
  • Calorie Calculator in pure JS — no sign-in, no backend
  • Mobile-first layout with an accessible drop-down menu
  • Personal Progress saved locally for privacy (no data leaves your device)

View site ↗

*"Negative-calorie" foods are better described as low-energy-density foods. They don’t literally burn more calories than they contain, but they can support fullness with fewer calories.
